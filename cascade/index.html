Born in 1988, Pascal Hien is a product designer from Germany. He has a diploma degree in Industrial Design from the University of Arts Berlin. He spent one year studying at ENSCI les Ateliers and worked for Arik Levy design studio in Paris. In 2014 he got a scholarship at Fabrica where he worked in the design studio. His work is based on a long research into materials, production techniques and innovations - eager to cross boundaries, experiment and push ideas to the maximum. His work range consists of experimental concepts, finished products and installations in public spaces.
